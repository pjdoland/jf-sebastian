# K.I.T.T. Wake Word Model

The wake word model `hey_kitt.onnx` needs to be trained.

## Wake Phrase
**"Hey, KITT"** (pronounced like "kit")

## Training Instructions

Follow the wake word training guide in `docs/TRAIN_WAKE_WORDS.md` to create the model.

### Recommended Settings
- Wake phrase: "Hey KITT"
- Alternative pronunciations to include:
  - "Hey kit"
  - "Hey K.I.T.T."
  - "Hey Knight Industries"

### Note
KITT is an acronym for "Knight Industries Two Thousand" and is pronounced like the word "kit".

Once trained, place the `hey_kitt.onnx` file in this directory.
